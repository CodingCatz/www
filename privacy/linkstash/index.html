LinkStash 隱私權政策

最後更新：2026-06-20

LinkStash（以下稱「本擴充」）是一個 Chrome 待看連結書籤管理擴充套件。本政策說明本擴充如何處理您的資料。

1. 蒐集與儲存的資料

本擴充僅儲存您主動收存的資料：連結網址、頁面標題、您自訂的標籤與作者顯示名稱，以及介面偏好設定（排序、收合狀態等）。這些資料儲存於 Chrome 的 chrome.storage.sync，並透過您自己的 Google／Chrome 帳號在您的裝置間同步。

2. 資料傳輸

本擴充不會將您的資料傳送至開發者伺服器或任何第三方。唯一的外部網路請求發生於您收存 YouTube 影片時：本擴充會向 YouTube 官方 oEmbed 端點（https://www.youtube.com/oembed）傳送該影片網址，以取得其發布頻道名稱。此請求受 YouTube／Google 之隱私政策規範。

3. 頁面內容存取

當您主動收存某頁面時，本擴充會暫時讀取該分頁的標題與作者相關資訊（透過頁面 og 標籤或 DOM），用以建立書籤。本擴充不會在背景持續監聽或讀取您的瀏覽內容。

4. 資料分享與販售

本擴充不販售、不分享、不轉移您的任何資料，也不含任何追蹤、分析或廣告。

5. 資料刪除

您可在側欄刪除個別連結，或透過 chrome://extensions 移除本擴充以清除所有本機資料。

6. 聯絡方式

如有任何疑問，請來信：catz.software@gmail.com

7. 政策變更

本政策若有更新，將於本頁公布並更新上方日期。

LinkStash Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-20

LinkStash ("the Extension") is a Chrome read-it-later bookmark manager. This policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

1. Data we store

The Extension only stores data you actively save: link URLs, page titles, your custom tags and author display names, and interface preferences (sort order, collapse state, etc.). This data is stored in Chrome's chrome.storage.sync and synced across your devices through your own Google/Chrome account.

2. Data transmission

The Extension does not send your data to the developer's servers or any third party. The only external network request occurs when you save a YouTube video: the Extension sends that video URL to YouTube's official oEmbed endpoint (https://www.youtube.com/oembed) to retrieve its publishing channel name. This request is governed by YouTube's/Google's privacy policy.

3. Page content access

When you actively save a page, the Extension temporarily reads that tab's title and author-related information (via the page's og tags or DOM) to create the bookmark. The Extension does not continuously monitor or read your browsing in the background.

4. Data sharing and selling

The Extension does not sell, share, or transfer any of your data, and contains no tracking, analytics, or ads.

5. Data deletion

You can delete individual links in the side panel, or remove the Extension via chrome://extensions to clear all local data.

Questions: catz.software@gmail.com

7. Changes

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date above.
